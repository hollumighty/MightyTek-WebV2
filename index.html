ABOUT MIGHTY-TEK

We have what it takes to exceed your expectations.

We are a contract manufacturing company that specializes in Plastic Injection Moulding, Welding and Fabrication and Precision Metal Machinning.

Our commitment to excellence in customer service, innovation and quality has earned us a reputation for dependability. We don't just build their parts, we prefer to partner with them from design stage to help enhance their products for manufacturability. From engineering to electromechanical assembly we can take your idea from design to production.

We have an in-house team of specialists with required domain expertise, skills, and experience to get the job done. Our team specialize in many areas-including lean consulting, all kinds of mechanical manufacturing, project management and more, We also foster innovation and creativity, which allow our client to outspace their competition.
