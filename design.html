CAD Design

Mighty-Tek offers best in class computer-aided design (CAD) service with the latest softwares, experienced engineers with deep domain knowledge. We use in-house templates and best practices to deliver high quality work towards improvising each design feature to achieve high level of functionality and performance. All this development is seamlessly communicated to the customers to ensure that customer feedback is a part of the development process at all times.

This close collaboration helps us deliver your project in a very cost-efficient and effective way. Below subsections provide detailed information about our CAD services

2D Drafting

2D drafting is used in fully defining requirements of products so as to convey all the required information that will allow a manufacturer produce that component with all the necessary design features.
Our knowledge in drafting tools and modelling helps us to produce very accurate representation of your 3D model with all necessary sectional views and tolerances to achieve flawless manufacturing.

3D Modeling.

3D modeling is the process of developing a three-dimensional computer-aided design (CAD) model of a product or process using a computer softwares.

We offer full range of 3D modeling services, which includes but not limited to: solid and surface modelling, sheet metal design, assembly for both parametric and non-parametric models.
We use best practices to achieve consistent high-quality drawings, which make it easy for interpretation and executions for our customers.

Machine Design.

Machine design has several key steps including detail consideration of all the machine operations, developing concepts, generating CAD models, optimizing using CAE, and ensuring the feasibility of high volume production.
We use CAD animations to show the exact motion of the machine while it is in operation to ensure it fulfills all your requirements before going into production alongside we generate shop drawings with all neccessary details needed for fabrication.
